# Employee Onboarding Guide

This guide is for the first Glasswell field beta. Use the app exactly as instructed during testing so issues are easy to reproduce.

## Log In

1. Open the app link from the owner or manager.
2. Tap `Login`.
3. Enter the email and password provided for your employee account.
4. If the app says your profile is inactive or missing, stop and ask the owner or manager to finish setup.

## Install The PWA

### iPhone

1. Open the app in Safari.
2. Tap the share button.
3. Tap `Add to Home Screen`.
4. Open Glasswell from the new home screen icon.

### Android

1. Open the app in Chrome.
2. Tap the install prompt or the three-dot menu.
3. Tap `Install app` or `Add to Home screen`.
4. Open Glasswell from the app icon.

## Start A Shift

1. Open the map.
2. Tap `Start Shift`.
3. Allow location access if prompted.
4. Confirm the shift timer appears.

## Start A Route

1. After starting a shift, tap the route/navigation button.
2. Review the next recommended street.
3. Tap `Start next street` when you are ready to work that street.

## Start And Complete A Street

1. Start the street from the route or street drawer.
2. Confirm the street is locked to you.
3. Use the large `+` button after each house knocked.
4. Use quick result buttons after a knock.
5. Tap `Complete Street` when the street is finished.

## Create A Quick Lead

1. While on an active street, tap `Lead` or an interested quick result.
2. Add phone number if available.
3. Add name, quote, or notes if they are quick to capture.
4. Save the lead.

## Schedule A Follow-Up

1. Open a lead or customer.
2. Tap a follow-up preset such as Tomorrow, 3 Days, or 1 Week.
3. Confirm the follow-up appears in the customer or lead timeline.

## What Not To Do During Beta

- Do not delete or reset data unless the owner asks you to.
- Do not mark a street `Do Not Knock` unless the customer or property clearly requires it.
- Do not override another employee's street lock.
- Do not use Cloud Mode if the app shows connection warnings unless the owner is testing sync.
- Do not rely on the app as the only record for payment, invoices, or payroll during beta.
- Report confusing screens immediately through `More` -> `Report Issue / Feedback`.
